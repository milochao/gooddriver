How It Works

Think of it like crowdfunding, but structured. Everyone participates. When someone in the club has a verified repair, members share a small portion that week. When nothing happens, nothing comes out.

Rules keep it fair:

You keep your liability insurance. Required. Separate.

You get a quote for your six-month plan. Based on your car and history.

You pay about 20% upfront. Service fee.

The rest stays with you. When repairs happen, a share comes out.

Weekly cap. Six-month cap.

Worst case: you have a repair event, pay your full share. That's roughly what you'd have paid a regular insurer anyway.

Difference is, you only pay that if something actually happens.
