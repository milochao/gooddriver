How It Works

You get a quote based on your car and your record. Then you pay 20% upfront to join. The other 80% stays in your bank account — we only pull from it when there's a repair to share.

1.

You keep your liability insurance. Required by law. Separate from this.

2.

You get a quote for a six-month plan based on your car and driving record.

3.

You pay 20% upfront as a service fee.

4.

Each week, if there are repairs to cover, you contribute a small share. If there aren't, you contribute nothing.

5.

Your contributions are capped. You'll never pay more than your quoted plan amount over six months.

We don't hold your money in an account. Repairs are funded by members when they happen. What you don't use stays yours.
